What is a UPI QR code?

A UPI QR code is a square barcode that contains your UPI payment information. When scanned with a UPI-enabled app, it automatically fills in your payment details, making transactions quick and easy.

Is this UPI QR code generator free to use?

Yes, our UPI QR code generator is completely free to use with no hidden costs or limitations.

Which UPI apps support these QR codes?

Our QR codes work with all major UPI apps including PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay, and any other app that supports UPI payments.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! Your data is processed entirely in your browser and is never stored on our servers. This ensures complete privacy and security for your payment information.

Can I use these QR codes for my business?

Yes, these QR codes are perfect for businesses of all sizes. You can print them, display them on your website, or share them digitally with customers.

What information is stored in the QR code?

The QR code contains your UPI ID, name (if provided), amount (if specified), and transaction note (if added). This information is encoded in a standardized UPI format that payment apps can read.

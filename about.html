About Us

UPI QR Generator is a free online tool made to help anyone generate UPI payment QR codes instantly. It works with PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM UPI and all UPI supported apps.

Who We Are

We built UPI QR Generator to make digital payments easier for everyone. Whether you are a shop owner, freelancer, student, or small business — you can create your own UPI QR code within seconds and start receiving payments.

This tool runs completely in your browser, which means your details are not stored on our servers. We believe in simple, fast and privacy-friendly tools.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple and free solution for generating UPI QR codes that works on every device, without requiring any app installation or technical knowledge.
